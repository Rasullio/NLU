# NLU App
This repository is my individual portfolio.
Our goal is to solve the main issues that many NLU students have with the app.
It also includes the problem being solved, the system vision, my role, and course artifacts. 

The vision is for the app to be more reliable, clear, and easy for students to navigate.

#My role on the team
My role is Software Developer: I am responsible for developing the apps for the group

For our final project in Week 8, our team put together a System Pitch Deck aimed at making the digital experience at National Louis University much better. We really focused on building a Unified Student Portal to help fix common frustrations like confusing navigation, login errors, and those long wait times for support that students and faculty often deal with.

The new system we came up with includes some really useful features:

We designed a central dashboard so everyone can find their classes and campus services in one spot.

We added guided support tools to make it easier for people to troubleshoot technical issues on their own.

We simplified the whole workflow for reporting and tracking school-related problems.

We made sure the portal experience feels the same no matter which NLU system you are logged into.

Overall, our plan shows how using a modular design and modern technology can actually work with the school's current setup to make the whole digital environment feel a lot more seamless.
